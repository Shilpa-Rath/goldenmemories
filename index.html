OUR FEATURES

A Beautiful Garden: Celebrate your special occasion in a beautiful garden. Our lawn is covered with soft grass that is cushy even to walk barefoot. Our flowery bowers and European decor of plants provide you ample photographic opportunities.

Air-Conditioned Rooms: The RJ Lawns features fully air-conditioned rooms for both the bride and the groom. We also have an air-conditioned reception hall to host your guests. Forget those sweaty overcrowded rooms which leave you and your guests uncomfortable, book RJ Lawn for your mandap in Bhubaneswar for marriage.


Charming Illuminations: Our garden comes alive every night with magical lighting. The sparkling colors of our creatively placed LEDs illuminate the green of the garden like a passage out of a fairy-tale. Filling the venue with an aura of glamour, celebrations & photogenicity.
